About Me

Greetings! and welcome to my portfolio. I have been in IT for many years looking from the sidelines as a Business Analyst, Data Analyst, and Project Manager, always implementing and directing tehcnology, but very rarely building it.

I am excited to present to you what I have actually built with the knowledge gained from the Georgia tech Bootcamp coding certification. This process has helped me to better understand the challenges that developers and engineers go through on a daily basis.

I manage a team of 7 Database Developers scattered across the country, and after completing this certification I hope to be able to understand and direct my team more efficiently. I also hope to be able to complete some work myself.
